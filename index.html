Frequently Asked Questions

How does the matching algorithm work?
Our proprietary matching algorithm uses over 100 different data points to create perfect matches between candidates and positions. It analyzes skills, experiences, culture fit, and career trajectory to find the optimal match.
Is FACH HR free for students?
Yes! Students can use our basic service completely free, with up to 3 applications per week. Premium plans are available for additional features.
How do I get started as an employer?
Simply create an account, post your first position, and start receiving qualified candidate matches. You can try our starter plan for free.
Which universities are included in your talent pool?
We currently focus on elite Swiss universities including ETH Zurich, EPFL, and HSG, with plans to expand to additional institutions soon.
